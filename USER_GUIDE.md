# User Guide - Ticket Booking Flow

## Complete User Journey

### 1. Browse Events (Home Page)

- View upcoming events
- Click on event to see details

### 2. Select Date & Time (Event Page)

- Choose preferred date
- Select time slot
- Click "Continue" to seat selection

### 3. Choose Seats (Seat Selection Page)

- View venue map with sections
- Click available seats to select
- See ticket pricing by tier (VIP, Standard, Economic)
- Review selected tickets
- See subtotal and service fees
- Click "Checkout"

### 4. Complete Payment (Checkout Page)

**Progress**: Step 3 - Checkout

- **Left Side**: Review ticket list
  - Event image and details
  - Section, row, seat information
  - Individual prices
- **Right Side**: Payment details
  - Select payment method (PayPal/Credit Card)
  - Enter payment information
  - See pricing summary
  - Agree to privacy policy
  - Click "Pay" button
- **Countdown Timer**: 5:12 to complete purchase

### 5. Download Ticket (Success Page)

**Progress**: Step 4 - Get Ticket

#### What You'll See

- ✅ "Payment Successful!" confirmation
- Congratulations message
- Purchase summary
- Customer details
- Special rewards unlocked

#### Your Ticket

- Beautiful ticket card with event image
- Ticket quantity and section
- Seat numbers
- Date and time

#### Actions You Can Take

**Download Ticket**

1. Click "Download Ticket" button
2. Wait for image generation (shows loading state)
3. Ticket saved as PNG image to your device
4. Filename: `Event-Name-Ticket.png`

**Share Ticket**

1. Click "Share Ticket" button
2. On mobile: Native share menu appears
   - Share via SMS, WhatsApp, Email, etc.
3. On desktop: Ticket details copied to clipboard
4. Includes: Event name, date, time, section, seat

2. **Checkout Timer**: Complete payment within 5 minutes
3. **Download**: Save ticket immediately after purchase

## Troubleshooting

**Can't download ticket?**

- Check browser permissions
- Try different browser
- Use desktop if on mobile

**Share not working?**

- Check clipboard permissions
- Manually copy ticket details
- Take screenshot as alternative

**Lost purchase data?**

- Don't refresh success page
- Check downloads folder
- Contact support with payment confirmation
